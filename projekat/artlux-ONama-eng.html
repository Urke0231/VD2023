About us

ARTLUX Gallery is a contemporary art gallery located in New Belgrade. ARTLUX Gallery focuses on thematic exhibitions and showcasing many local and international artists both emerging and established.

We believe that creativity should be the focus of the gallery programming, and believes in Art for the sake of Art while bringing the community together. We are a proud partner of ASID (American Society of Interior Designers) & LCDQ (La Cienega Design Quarter).
